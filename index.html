Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our CPL program

What is CPL and how does it work?

CPL stands for Cost Per Lead. When you apply through our platform and get matched with a premium employer, we earn a commission for connecting qualified candidates with opportunities. This doesn't affect your application or potential earnings.

How much can I earn with CPL opportunities?

Our CPL rates range from $85 to $150 per qualified lead, depending on your experience and the specific role. High-demand positions like software development typically command the highest CPL rates.

Is there any cost to apply?

No, applying through our platform is completely free for job seekers. We only earn when you get matched with one of our premium employer partners.

How long does the application process take?

Our streamlined application process takes approximately 5-10 minutes to complete. Once submitted, you'll receive instant feedback on your CPL qualification status.
